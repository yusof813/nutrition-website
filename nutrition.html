more information

Exercise is any physical activity that helps improve and maintain overall health, fitness, and well-being. It includes a variety of activities such as cardio exercises like running, swimming, and cycling, strength training like push-up, squat, and weightlifting, as well as flexibility and relaxation activities like yoga and stretching. Regular exercise strengthens the heart, muscles, and bones, improves blood circulation, and helps control body weight. It also has mental health benefits, such as reducing stress, anxiety, and symptoms of depression while boosting mood and confidence. In addition, staying active can lower the risk of serious health conditions like Type 2 Diabetes, Heart Disease, and obesity. Exercise can also improve sleep quality, increase energy levels, and enhance brain function, including memory and concentration. For the best results, it is recommended that adults aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise each week along with regular strength training, while also maintaining a balanced diet and proper rest.
