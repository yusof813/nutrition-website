extra information

What is Nutrition? Nutrition is the study and practice of how food affects the body. It’s about providing the right nutrients your body needs to grow, repair itself, and function properly. Good nutrition supports energy, immunity, brain function, and overall health. Main Nutrients and Their Benefits Carbohydrates Main source of energy Found in rice, bread, pasta, oats, fruits, and vegetables Proteins Helps build and repair muscles and tissues Found in meat, fish, eggs, beans, lentils, nuts, and seeds Fats Provide energy and support brain and heart health Found in oils, nuts, seeds, avocado, and fatty fish Focus on healthy fats and limit trans fats Vitamins Support immunity, vision, skin, and growth Found in fruits, vegetables, dairy, and fortified foods Minerals Help bones, teeth, blood, and nerve function Examples: calcium (dairy, leafy greens), iron (meat, beans), potassium (bananas, potatoes) Fiber Improves digestion and prevents constipation Found in whole grains, fruits, vegetables, and legumes Water Essential for all body functions Helps digestion, temperature regulation, and nutrient transport
